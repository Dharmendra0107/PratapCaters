About Us

A Legacy of Flavour Born in Lucknow

At Pratap Caters, we bring the rich culinary heritage of Awadh to your most special moments. Founded over 15 years ago in the heart of Lucknow, we have earned the trust of thousands of families, corporates, and event planners across Uttar Pradesh.

Our master chefs blend traditional Nawabi recipes with contemporary techniques, ensuring every dish tells a story of craftsmanship and care. From intimate gatherings to grand weddings of 5,000 guests — we deliver nothing short of excellence.
